Our Founder

The foundation was laid to the Siddhartha school in June 1981 by educationists and philanthropists of Vellore town who founded themselves into a registered body known as “People’s Progressive Trust”, with Mr. R.P. Ulaganambi, M.A.,B.L., Former Member of Parliament as Managing Trustee.

ESTABLISHED 1981

Siddhartha Senior Secondary School, Vellore in 1981. FIRST CBSE SCHOOL IN VELLORE DISTRICT WITH LOWEST TUITION FEES.

STUDENTS: 500

The Nursery, Primary and Secondary sections of the school were started with modest strength of 85 students on 5 June 1981 by “The people’s progressive society”.

GRADUATION RATE 100%

We develop a sense of National Integration among our students.

LOCATION: MAIN SCHOOL

No.30, Vasantham Nagar, Phase 3, Sathuvachari, Vellore - 9.

TEACHER-STUDENT: 1:8

The school follows the central pattern of education formulated by the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi.
